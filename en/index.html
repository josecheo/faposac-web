    Facilidad Portuaria SAC is a family business, whose core business is maritime agency, stevedoring services in merchant ships, as well as handling and mobilization of merchandise and equipment in port and extra- port terminals, with more tan 25 years of experience in the sector. It is present in the ports: Matarani, Mollendo, Ilo, La Planchada, Callao, Salaverry and Chimbote.


    The main focus of Facilidad Portuaria SAC is to promote continous improvement in its internal processes and continue investing in resources that allow us to offer our clients comprehensive logistics meeting their needs and market demands.
